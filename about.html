About Me

My journey into development started with C++ and Data Structures & Algorithms, where I built a strong foundation in logical thinking and problem-solving. Understanding how things work at a core level shaped the way I approach every project — focusing not just on writing code, but writing efficient and structured solutions.


After mastering the fundamentals, I transitioned into Web Development, building responsive and interactive applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. I enjoy transforming ideas into clean, user-friendly interfaces that deliver real value.


Currently, I am strengthening my backend expertise with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js), working on authentication systems, RESTful APIs, and database-driven applications. My goal is to build scalable, secure, and production-ready applications — not just tutorial projects.


I am continuously improving my problem-solving skills, system design thinking, and full-stack development capabilities to grow into a well-rounded software engineer.
